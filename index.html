Paramount Plus Login



Paramount+ is a streaming service filled with popular shows, movies, and live sports. But before you can delve into the world of Yellowstone, Star Trek, and NFL football, you need to navigate the Paramount+ login process. It's simpler than you think!


Paramount Plus Login

  1. For Existing Subscribers:
    • Open the Paramount+ app on your TV.
    • Choose “Sign In” and select “On my TV.”
    • Enter the email address and password associated with your Paramount+ subscription (not your Amazon credentials).
    • Click “Sign In.”
  2. Via a TV Provider:
    • Visit paramountplus.com/activate on your computer or mobile device.
    • Enter the Activation Code displayed on your TV screen.
    • You’ll be redirected to your TV Provider’s sign-in page. Log in using your TV provider username and password.

Welcome to Paramount Plus!

Once logged in, you'll be greeted by a vibrant and user-friendly interface, ready to unlock a world of entertainment:

  • Extensive Content Library: Explore a vast collection of movies and TV shows. From blockbuster hits and critically acclaimed classics to binge-worthy originals and popular current series, there's something for everyone.
  • Live TV (on select plans): Depending on your subscription tier, you might have access to live TV channels, including sporting events and breaking news.
  • Dedicated Kids' Section: Keep your little ones entertained with a safe and curated selection of age-appropriate content.
  • Search Function: Easily find specific movies, shows, or actors using the convenient search bar.

Forgot Your Password?

No worries! If you've forgotten your password, simply click on the "Forgot Password" link on the login page of "Paramount Plus Login". Follow the prompts to reset your password securely and regain access to your account.


Additional Tips:

  • Use a Strong Password: Choose a unique and secure password to protect your account.
  • Enable Two-Factor Authentication: For extra security, consider enabling two-factor authentication. This adds an extra layer of protection by requiring a code sent to your phone or email.
  • Check Your Billing Information: Make sure your billing information is up-to-date to avoid interruptions in your service.
  • Contact Customer Support: If you encounter any issues with the login process or have questions, reach out to Paramount+ customer support for assistance.
